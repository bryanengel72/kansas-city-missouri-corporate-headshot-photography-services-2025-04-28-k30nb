Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear for my headshot session?

We recommend solid colors and professional attire that reflects your industry. Dark blues, grays, and neutral tones work well. Avoid busy patterns or bright colors that might distract from your face. We'll provide a detailed preparation guide upon booking.

How long does a typical session take?

Standard sessions last 30-45 minutes, allowing time for multiple poses and outfit changes. We ensure a relaxed atmosphere while maintaining efficiency. Executive sessions can be arranged for up to 90 minutes.

What's included in the final delivery?

You'll receive professionally retouched images in multiple formats, optimized for various platforms (LinkedIn, website, print). Package includes both high-resolution and web-optimized versions, delivered within 3-5 business days.

Do you offer group corporate rates?

Yes, we offer special rates for corporate teams of 5 or more. We can arrange on-site sessions at your office and ensure consistent styling across your team's photos.

What about retouching and editing?

Our professional retouching service includes skin smoothing, color correction, and subtle enhancements while maintaining a natural look. Additional retouching services are available upon request.
